Booking Policy

Deposit Guidelines


  • Send your deposit to hajahawad@gmail.com
  • Include name, appointment date, and selected
    hairstyle in the email.
  • The deposit will be deducted from the service
    price.
  • Ensure you have the remaining payment ready
    on the day of your appointment.

Appointment Preparation


  • Ensure your hair is fully washed and blow-dried
    prior to your appointment.
  • Braiding hair or extension must be pre-stretched.
  • Book according to your schedule, as we will not
    rush my work.
  • Notify us 24-48 hours in advance if you wish to
    change your selected hairstyle.
  • Communicate promptly if you are running late.

Rescheduling and Cancellations


  • To reschedule, please notify us at least 24 hours
    in advance.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance
    will result in the loss of your deposit.
  • New deposit will be required to book another
    appointment.
