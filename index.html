Booking Policy

Deposit Guidelines
Please send your deposit to hajahawad@gmail.com
Include your name, appointment date, and selected hairstyle in the email.
The deposit will be deducted from the service price.
Ensure that you have the remaining payment ready on the day of your appointment.
Preparation for Your Appointment
Ensure your hair is fully washed and blow-dried prior to your appointment.
Braiding hair must be pre-stretched.
Please book according to your schedule, as I will not rush my work.
Notify us 24-48 hours in advance if you wish to change your selected hairstyle.
Communicate promptly if you are running late.
Rescheduling and Cancellations
To reschedule, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance.
Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will result in the loss of your deposit.
A new deposit will be required to book another appointment.
Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
